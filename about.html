Philosophy of Work

The goal of great design is not to be noticed, it's to be felt, experienced. It's about removing friction for the people who depend on it, so they can get what they need and get back to the work that matters.

Why Enterprise & Industrial

Complex environments have always made sense to me. I've spent my life wanting to understand why someone would do something a certain way, I'd be curious of what drives their behavior, what do they actually need? Enterprise and industrial design gave me the hardest version of that problem: users under pressure, systems with real consequences, and no room for friction.
